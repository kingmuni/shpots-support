Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new spot?

Tap the "+" button on the main screen, then add photos or videos, notes, a title, and location for your spot.

How does iCloud sync work?

iCloud sync can be enabled manually in the Settings tab. When enabled, your spots will sync across all your devices signed in with the same Apple ID.

Can I share my spots with others?

Yes! Use the share button on any spot or collection to send the location and details to friends. Note: Images are not currently included when sharing, but this feature will be available in future updates.

How do I organize my spots into collections?

Create collections to group related spots together, like "Favorite Restaurants" or "Weekend Getaways".

How do I delete a spot or collection?

Swipe left on any spot or collection in the list view to reveal the delete option.
